Our Site

Our purpose of The BJ Taylor Counseling, Cancer and Resource Center is to serve and support women diagnosed with diverse cancers, by providing Biblical Counseling and assistance in locating the best resources.
We offer Biblical Counseling through our dedicated, certified Biblical Counselors.

What Is Biblical Counseling?

Biblical Counseling is using doctrinal truths from God's Word and practically applying those truths to everyday life, to help deal with life's problems and concerns. It's using God's Word as a guide to help you in your daily walk (John 17:17, Romans 8:28-29)
